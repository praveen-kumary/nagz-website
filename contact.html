Quick Answers

COMMON QUESTIONS.

How does the free call work?

It's a 20-minute conversation — no pitch, no pressure. We talk about where you are, what you've tried, and whether coaching makes sense for you.

What does the 12-week programme include?

A custom nutrition plan, personalised training programme, weekly check-ins with Nagz directly, and real-time adjustments based on your progress.

Do I need a gym?

No. The programme is built around what you have — full gym, home setup, or even 30-minute windows between calls.

How quickly will I see results?

Most clients feel a noticeable shift within the first week. Measurable body composition changes typically show by week 3-4.

How many clients do you take at a time?

Intake is deliberately small. Every client works directly with Nagz — no assistants, no automated plans. When spots are full, they're full.
